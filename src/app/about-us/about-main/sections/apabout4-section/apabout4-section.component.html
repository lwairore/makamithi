Farmer's choice

Makamithi Agrovet is the leading distributor and retailer of farming products in the Ukambani region. Covering Machakos, Makueni and Kitui Counties. We currently have 5 retail branches in Machakos, Emali and Wote Town. All branches have a qualified agronomist and veterinary specialist to support local farmers, ensuring they carry out sustainable farming practices. Our wholesales & distribution fuction operates from Machakos Town and delivers across the region.
